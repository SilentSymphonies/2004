Causes and Impact

The Earthquake happened due to a tectonic subduction zone. The culprit behind this is the India and Oceanic submerging into the water. The earthquake was vertically displaced on seafloor which is the cause of something known as inter-plate thrust fault. Which is basically the contacts of surface with overriding plate and underthrusting plates in convergent margins. That is what made the earthquake so deadly.

Global

Responses

Many organizations started working on new technology.

The first big invention was a new tsunami detector. which detects ocean bottom pressure that can be caused by tsunami

Old tsunameter has been improved by being placed one hour of tsunami travel time from seismic zones. To Prevent their sensors from overwhelmed by the intensity of earthquake’s seismic waves

A new noise filtering system designed by the NOAA allows placing tsunameters 10 minutes away from earthquake source.

Sea Level Stations are a lot cheaper than before(5000$)

GOOS now developed a website for “coordinating and promoting the sharing of real time data
